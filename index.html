Francielle Vargas

E-Mail | Google Scholar | ORCID | GitHub | Linkedin | Lattes | Curriculum Vitae |

I am a computer and language scientist with an M.Sc. and Ph.D. (final year) in Natural Language Processing. During my Ph.D, I was a visiting scientist at the University of Southern California (2024). I received my M.Sc. in Computer Science and Computational Mathematics from the University of São Paulo (2017). Previously, I obtained a B.S. in Information Systems and a B.A. in Linguistics. I am interested in Natural Language Processing, Machine Learning and Computational Social Science. My research focus on improvement the explainability, robustness, and fairness of language models. Hence, I rely on machine learning techniques including neural networks to design and guide the development of safer, trustworthy and socially responsible human language technologies.

Research Interests

  • hate speech and offensive language detection, radicalism, bias mitigation, fairness
  • opinion and argument mining, emotion, sentiment and stylistic analysis, subjectivity
