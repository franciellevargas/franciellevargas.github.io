Factual Reasoning and Discourse-Aware Computational Resources for Fake News Detection

In the last few years, there was a growth in the number of web and social media users. Consequently, the potential of misinformation also increased, resulting in the production of fake news, fake reviews (also known as opinion spam), deceptive discussion, simple lies, etc. Fake news detection is defined as the prediction of the chances of a particular news article being intentionally deceptive. Moreover, stements that are intentionally misstated (or manipulated) are of considerable interest to researchers, government, security, and financial systems.

In this project, we propose to investigate and development of discourse aware computational methods and factual reasoning for fake news detection on the web and social media, which may be applied to different languages.
